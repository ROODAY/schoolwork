Who

40 male participants between the ages of 20 and 50 in the New Haven Area

What

The willingness of participants to shock 'learners.'

When

1961

Where

Yale University

Why

To observe the effect of authority on obedience.

How

Participants were told to ask 'learners' questions, and if the learners answered wrong, they were shocked.

Experiment Objective

To see if authority affected the level of obedience in an individual.

Type of Study

The Milgram Experiments were non-randomized experiments that always put participants in the teacher position to see how they responded to their instructions.
