What Even Is This Thing

This is a website made by Rudhra Raveendran, Gavin Mays, Sam Neil, and Serge Piskun for extra credit in Mrs. Mills' 2015-2016 AP Physics class. It contains notes from the textbook, particularly from sections 2.1 through 2.6, showing that we really did pay attention. This was definitely not done the night before. We promise.

How Much Extra Credit Does This Deserve?

If we were teachers, we'd probably give a letter grade. ;) But granted, we're not teachers, so realistically, probably closer to a third of a letter grade.
